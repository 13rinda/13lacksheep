Hi, I am 13rinda

Founder & Owner

Hi there, I'm Brinda Rebekah Mailvaganam, the founder of 13lacksheep.I embarked on the journey of creating 13lacksheep due to the struggle in finding an Airbnb that caters to intimate gatherings in Montreal. Finding a place that not only accommodates a large group but also provides ample space for activities was a daunting task. To address the complexities of space and sleeping arrangements, I innovated custom booth beds—smartly designed to transform into comfortable floor mattresses or fold into a cozy sofa booth. These signature booth beds, featured across all my properties, comfortably accommodate groups ranging from 10 to 21 guests. You can explore the unique design here. As a Software Engineer my world thrives on innovation and creativity. This perspective fuels my approach to 13lacksheep, aiming to offer a singular, all-inclusive space where delightful memories are crafted with loved ones. Understanding the stress of event planning, my goal is to provide clients with a seamless guest experience, ensuring they step into a rental fully equipped with decor, cake, snacks, sound systems, and more—all ready for their unforgettable moments.
